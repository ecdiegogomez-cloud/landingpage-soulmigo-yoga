# Why Amazon First — Not Your Own Website

## The Core Problem

When launching a new product with a limited budget, you have one scarce resource: **time and money**. The question isn't «which is better?» in the abstract, but **«which generates sales fastest with the least investment?»**

For Soulmigo at this stage, the answer is Amazon.

---

## Amazon: The Sales Machine Already Built For You

### 1. Purchase Intent vs. Interruption

On Amazon, someone searches **«yoga socks uk»** — they already want to buy.

On Instagram or TikTok ads, you interrupt someone scrolling through cat videos. You then have to:

1. Grab their attention
2. Convince them they need the product
3. Drive them to your website
4. Get them to trust a brand they've never heard of
5. Get them to actually buy

That's **5 barriers** instead of 1.

### 2. Built-In Trust

Amazon Prime, easy returns, secure payments — the customer already trusts Amazon, they don't need to trust you yet. On a new website, that trust has to be built from scratch and takes months.

### 3. Free Organic Traffic

A well-optimised Amazon listing shows up in searches without paying for ads. A new website reaches nobody without:

- **Organic SEO:** takes 6–12 months minimum to rank
- **Google Ads:** £500–£2,000/month for e-commerce

### 4. Speed to First Sale

| Channel | Time to first sale |
|---------|-------------------|
| Amazon (optimised listing) | 48–72 hours |
| Own website + SEO | 6–12 months |
| Own website + paid ads | 1–3 months (with high budget) |

## The Right Sequence

This doesn't mean a website is a bad idea — it means there is a **correct order**:

```
STAGE 1 (Now)
Amazon → Generates sales and reviews without high investment
Organic TikTok → Free advertising, drives traffic to Amazon

STAGE 2 (Once you have income)
Own website → Use Amazon profits to fund it
Email list → Built organically from TikTok followers

STAGE 3 (Once you have a brand)
Paid ads → Retargeting works when people already know you
Paid influencers → When you have the budget
```

### Why This Sequence Works

- **Amazon validates the product:** If it doesn't sell on Amazon where traffic is already in buying mode, it won't sell from a cold-traffic website either.
- **Amazon funds the website:** First months' profits pay for the site, SEO, and eventually ads.
- **Organic TikTok closes the loop:** Drives warm traffic (people who already know the product) to Amazon, improving your organic ranking on the platform.

## Conclusion

> A website without traffic is an empty shop on a back alley.
> Amazon is a shop in the busiest shopping centre in the country — and the rent is a commission on what you actually sell.

For Soulmigo right now:

- **Low available budget** → Amazon is the only channel that can deliver positive returns from month 1
- **No existing audience** → Amazon already has the audience
- **No brand trust yet** → Amazon lends its trust while you build yours

The website and paid ads will come — but at the right moment.
