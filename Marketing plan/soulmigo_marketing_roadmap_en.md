# Soulmigo Yoga Socks — Marketing Roadmap (Low-Budget Edition)

A step-by-step guide for someone with no marketing experience and minimal investment.

**Product:** Premium Yoga Socks (Organic Cotton + Anatomical Grip)
**Brand:** Soulmigo Ltd. — Glasgow, Scotland, UK
**Primary channels:** Amazon UK → Organic TikTok → Website (only when funds allow)
**Target Launch:** 1 September 2026
**Today:** 1 March 2026
**Time Available:** ~6 months

> **Low-budget philosophy:** Free first, paid only when it generates a return. Amazon is priority #1 because it sells for you. TikTok is your free advertising. The website can wait.

---

## CHANNEL FRAMEWORK (ORB)

Structure all your presence across 3 channel types. The goal is to funnel everything into owned channels.

| Type | What it is | Soulmigo examples |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Owned** | You control it. Nobody can take it away. | Email list (Mailchimp) |
| **Rented** | You have visibility but the algorithm decides. | TikTok, Instagram |
| **Borrowed** | Someone else's audience you can tap temporarily. | Yoga micro-influencers, yoga communities on Reddit/FB |

**Golden rule:** Use TikTok and influencers to drive people to your email list. Email is yours forever.

## PHASE 0: MINIMUM FOUNDATIONS (1–15 March 2026)

**Goal:** Set up only what is strictly necessary. No unnecessary spending.

### 0.1 Secure Your Brand Name (Week 1 — 1–8 Mar)

- **What:** Register your free accounts before someone else takes them.
- **How:**
  1. Register TikTok: `@soulmigo` — **Free. Do this today.**
  2. Register Instagram: `@soulmigo` — **Free.**
  3. Buy domain `soulmigo.co.uk` — only if you have £10–£15 available (Namecheap).
  4. Business email: **use free Gmail** (`soulmigo.brand@gmail.com`) until you have income. Google Workspace (£5/month) can wait.
  5. **UK Trademark (~£170): DEFER.** Not urgent to start selling. Do it once you have stable income.
- **Cost:** £0–£15 (domain only if you can)
- **Time:** 1 hour

### 0.2 Define Your Brand Voice — Brand Psychology (Week 1 — 1–8 Mar)

- **What:** Write down in 1 page how Soulmigo communicates.
- **How:** Answer these 4 questions in a document:
  1. **Target customer:** Women 25–45, yoga/pilates, value sustainability.
  2. **Tone:** Warm, honest, grounded. Not corporate.
  3. **3 values:** Sustainability, Craftsmanship, Mindful Movement.
  4. **Differentiator:** 3-zone anatomical grip, organic cotton, 24% cheaper than Sweaty Betty.
- **Cost:** Free
- **Time:** 1–2 hours
- **Psychology applied:** People buy based on *identity*, not just function. "I'm the kind of person who chooses sustainable things" is more powerful than any technical feature. Your brand must reflect that.

### 0.3 Minimum Brand Kit (Week 2 — 9–15 Mar)

- **What:** Have logo and colours ready to use everywhere.
- **How:** Use **free Canva** to create:
  1. Post template for TikTok/Instagram (9:16 and 1:1)
  2. Document with hex colour codes and font names
  3. Logo in PNG (white background and transparent background)
- **Don't hire a designer now.** Canva is enough to start.
- **Cost:** Free
- **Time:** 2–3 hours

## PHASE 1: SUPPLIER AND PRODUCT (March–August 2026)

**Goal:** Get the best product at the lowest cost possible.

### 1.1 Contact Suppliers (9–31 March 2026)

- **What:** Send your brief and get quotes from 3–5 factories.
- **How:**
  1. Use your existing `supplier_contact_draft_en.md` document.
  2. Contact suppliers on Alibaba (free). Reference: Zhuji Zhongqi.
  3. Request: price per unit, MOQ, lead time, sample cost, certifications.
  4. **Negotiate the MOQ** — ask if they accept 50 units for the first order.
- **Cost:** Free
- **Time:** 2–4 weeks for responses

### 1.2 Samples (April 2026 — estimated receipt: mid-April)

- **What:** Order samples from your best supplier (maximum 2).
- **How:**
  1. Order sample from your favourite supplier (~£30–£50 sample cost).
  2. Test yourself: grip, softness, wash x5. Ask 3 yoga-practising friends for feedback.
  3. Don't spend on multiple samples if one supplier already stands out.
- **Cost:** £30–£50 (maximum)
- **Time:** 2–3 weeks

### 1.3 Production Order (May 2026 — estimated delivery: August)

- **What:** Place the smallest possible order for the first launch.
- **Low-budget strategy:**
  - **Start with 50–100 pairs total** (no more). Spread across 2–3 colours, not all.
  - Validate that it sells before ordering more. Reduces risk.
  - Pay 30% deposit, the rest against shipping.
- **Cost:** ~£111–£220 (product) + ~£100–£150 (air freight, customs, basic packaging)
- **Time:** 30–45 days production

### 1.4 DIY Photography — Amazon Photos (August 2026, on product receipt)

- **What:** Take your own photos with your phone.
- **Why:** A good photographer costs £150–£300. With your phone and technique you can achieve 80% of the result.
- **How:**
  1. **White background:** Buy a large white card from a stationery shop (~£2). Amazon requirement.
  2. **Light:** Film next to a window on a cloudy day (diffused light, no harsh shadows).
  3. **Free app:** Use Lightroom Mobile (free) for brightness/contrast adjustments.
  4. **7 photos needed for Amazon:**
     - Product on white background (main image)
     - Close-up of the anatomical grip
     - Socks on feet during a yoga pose
     - Infographic with the 3 grip zones (made in Canva, free)
     - Size chart (Canva, free)
     - Packaging: mesh bag + belly band
     - Visual comparison: Soulmigo vs. generic socks
- **Cost:** £2 (white card) — essentially free
- **Time:** 1 day

## PHASE 2: ORGANIC TIKTOK (May 2026 — ongoing)

**Goal:** Build an audience without spending anything. TikTok is your free advertising.

### 2.1 Set Up Profile (1–7 May 2026)

- Create `@soulmigo` account, switch to Business Account (free).
- Bio: "Organic cotton yoga socks 🌿 Anatomical grip. Glasgow, UK." + link to your Instagram or Amazon (when ready).
- **Cost:** Free — **Time:** 30 minutes

> **Content face:** The target audience is women aged 25–45 in the UK. Content converts significantly better when presented by a woman. Search Instagram/TikTok using: `#yogauk` `#yogateacheruk` `#pilatesuk` `#yogagirl`. Target: accounts with **1K–10K followers** in the UK who post yoga regularly. Offer free socks in exchange for honest content.

### 2.1b Pre-Launch Waitlist (May–August 2026)

Before launching on Amazon, build a minimal waitlist to generate anticipation.

- Create a Google Form (free) to collect emails.
- Message: "Soulmigo launches in September. Join for early access and launch pricing."
- Share the link in every TikTok and in your Instagram bio.
- **Why it works:** Perceived scarcity increases desire. A waitlist creates the feeling that it's worth waiting for.
- **Goal:** 50–100 emails before launch — those are your first buyers.
- **Cost:** Free

### 2.2 Content Strategy — 3 Pillars (from May 2026 onwards)

**Pillar 1 — Product Showcase (40%)**

- Close-up of grip on yoga mat
- Unboxing the mesh bag packaging
- Slow-motion grip test
- Your socks vs. normal socks comparison

**Pillar 2 — Educational (40%)**

- "Why your yoga socks keep slipping" (simple biomechanics)
- "What to look for in yoga socks" (materials, grip, fit)
- "Organic cotton vs synthetic for your skin"
- "How to wash grip socks so they last"

**Pillar 3 — Behind the Scenes (20%)**

- Receiving factory samples
- Packing first orders from Glasgow
- Grip design process (show sketches or design files)

### 2.3 TikTok Production Rules (applies from May 2026 onwards)

1. **Hook in first 2 seconds.** Example: "I spent 6 months designing the perfect yoga grip"
2. **Videos 15–45 seconds.** Shorter = more views when starting out.
3. **Film vertically (9:16)** with your phone. No extra equipment.
4. **Natural light.** Window on a cloudy day.
5. **Text on screen** — many people watch without sound.
6. **3–5 hashtags:** mix broad (#yoga #pilates) with niche (#gripdesign #organicyoga).
7. **Post 3–5 times per week** minimum.

### 2.4 TikTok Hook Formulas

The first second decides if the video lives or dies. These formulas are proven:

| Type | Formula | Soulmigo example |
|------|---------|------------------|
| Curiosity | "Nobody tells you this about [topic]" | "Nobody tells you this about yoga socks" |
| Contrarian | "Stop [common habit]" | "Stop buying yoga socks with random dots" |
| Story | "I spent [time] [doing something]" | "I spent 6 months designing the perfect grip" |
| Problem | "Why [common problem]" | "Why your yoga socks keep slipping" |
| Exclusive | "Only [X people] know this" | "Only yoga instructors know this" |

**Key rule:** Always start with the most eye-catching visual, not your face talking.

### 2.5 Free Growth Tactics

1. Comment genuinely on yoga creator videos with lots of followers.
2. Reply to your own comments with a video (TikTok boosts reply videos).
3. Use Duet/Stitch on trending yoga content.
4. Reply to comments within the first 30 minutes after posting.
5. Post at peak UK times: 7–9 AM, 12–2 PM, 7–9 PM.

### 2.6 Daily Engagement Routine (30 minutes)

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 5 min | Reply to all comments on your posts |
| 15 min | Comment on 5–10 yoga/pilates accounts (add genuine value, no promos) |
| 5 min | Duet or Stitch a trending yoga video |
| 5 min | Browse niche hashtags (#yogasocks #yogauk) and engage |

TikTok rewards active accounts. Every comment on someone else's video is a chance for them to discover your profile.

## PHASE 3: AMAZON UK (August–1 September 2026)

**Goal:** Launch on Amazon with minimum spend and maximum impact.

### 3.1 Account Type — Key Decision (August 2026)

| Type | Cost | When to use |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Individual** | £0/month (99p per sale) | If selling fewer than 40 units/month |
| **Professional** | £25/month | If selling more than 40 units/month |

- **Recommendation:** Start as an **Individual seller (free)**. Upgrade to Professional once you exceed 40 sales/month.
- **Brand Registry (~£170 trademark): DEFER.** You can sell without it. Register trademark once you have income.
- **Initial Cost:** £0
- **Time:** 1–3 days for approval

### 3.2 Optimised Listing — Most Important Thing (July–August 2026)

- **Title:** "Soulmigo Organic Cotton Yoga Socks - Anatomical 3-Zone Grip - Non-Slip Pilates Socks for Women - [Colour]"
- **5 Bullet Points:**
  1. Anatomical 3-zone grip (not random dots)
  2. 78% organic combed cotton — soft, breathable
  3. Seamless toe construction — no irritation during practice
  4. Includes reusable mesh wash bag
  5. Available in earth tones, sizes S/M/L
- **Description:** Brand story + detailed technical product info.
- **Backend keywords:** barre socks, non-slip socks, grip socks women, pilates grip socks, yoga grip socks cotton.
- **Cost:** Free (DIY) — **Time:** 1–2 days

### 3.3 First Reviews — Without Spending (August–September 2026)

1. **Insert card in packaging** (negligible cost): "Love your Soulmigo socks? Leave us an honest review on Amazon."
2. **Friends and family:** Have them buy at normal price and leave an honest review.
3. **Amazon 'Request a Review' button** — use it with every customer, it's free.
4. **Amazon Vine:** Only if you have Brand Registry. Defer for now.

- **Cost:** £0 — **Time:** Ongoing

### 3.4 Amazon PPC — Minimum Viable (Only after 5 reviews — Oct 2026)

- **Don't start with £10–£15/day.** That's £300–£450/month from the start.
- **Low-budget strategy:**
  1. Wait until you have at least 5 reviews before switching on PPC.
  2. Start with **£2–£3/day** on automatic campaign.
  3. Review every 2 weeks. Pause keywords that don't convert.
  4. Increase budget only with money from sales (not from your own pocket).
- **Cost:** £60–£90/month (starting small) vs. £450/month in the original plan
- **Time:** 20–30 minutes/week

### Influencer Seeding — Low-Cost Version

- **Don't send product to 10–20 people straight away.**
- **Start with 3–5 female micro-influencers** (1K–10K followers) in the UK.
- No contract, no payment. Just a product pack with a handwritten note.
- Track who posts. Build a relationship only with those who respond.
- **Reciprocity principle:** An unexpected, well-presented gift generates a natural desire to give back.
- **Cost:** ~£30–£50 (3 packs with shipping)

**Where to find them:**

| Platform | Search |
|----------|--------|
| Instagram | `#yogauk` `#yogateacheruk` `#pilatesuk` `#yogagirl` |
| TikTok | `#yogauk` `#pilatesuk` `#yogalife` |
| Billo.app | Post that you need a UGC creator for yoga socks |

**What to look for in their profile:**

- Regular yoga posts (minimum 2–3 times/week)
- Real comments from people (not just emojis)
- UK location visible in bio or stories

**Outreach message:**

> *"Hi! We're Soulmigo, a Glasgow-based brand launching organic cotton yoga socks with anatomical grip in the UK. We'd love to send you a complimentary pair — no obligation to post, but if you love them we'd be thrilled if you shared your honest thoughts. Would you be interested?"*

### Amazon Pricing Psychology

Pricing tactics at no extra cost:

- **Price anchor:** Set a "was" price of £18.99 at launch. The contrast makes £14.99 feel like a bargain.
- **The .99 rule:** £14.99 is perceived significantly cheaper than £15.
- **Price framing:** In the description write "less than £1/week if you use socks in your classes". Daily framing minimises perceived cost.
- **Competitor comparison:** "24% less than Sweaty Betty with superior anatomical grip" — anchoring against the competitor justifies your price.
