**Decision made:** Launch on Amazon UK on **1 September 2026**.\

## Why September is the Right Decision

- It is the **second biggest search peak of the year** in the UK for yoga and pilates (65% of the January maximum)
- **Less competition than January** — easier to rank organically with less PPC spend
- Gives you **3–4 months of reviews** before Black Friday and Christmas
- You arrive at **January 2027** (the absolute peak, 100%) with a mature, ranked listing
- It is the most realistic near-term date given a 60-day production cycle

### OCTOBER 2026 — Optimisation

**Goal:** Use real data to improve the listing.

- Review PPC every 2 weeks. Pause keywords with spend but no sales.
- Increase PPC budget **only with sales income**.
- Optimise bullet points with keywords generating actual clicks.
- Reply to all reviews publicly (builds trust with future buyers).
- Continue TikTok — start leaning into the "perfect yoga gift" angle.

---

### 27 NOVEMBER 2026 — Black Friday

**Goal:** Take advantage of peak traffic. By now you should have 10–20 reviews.

- Activate a 15–20% discount (Amazon lets you create free coupons).
- Strong TikTok push with gift angle: "the perfect yoga gift under £20".
- Temporarily increase PPC using accumulated sales income.
- Position the 2-pack with mesh bag as a **stocking filler**.

---

### DECEMBER 2026 — Christmas Gifting Season

**Goal:** Peak gifting period. Maintain sufficient stock.

- Check stock levels. If running low, contact factory for reorder.
- TikTok content: "gift ideas for people who do yoga".
- Keep PPC active with mature, optimised data.

---

### JANUARY 2027 — The Biggest Month of the Year

**Goal:** This is the best month of the entire year. You arrive prepared.

- Searches for "yoga socks uk" increase **200–300%** — the market is actively searching.
- By now you have: mature listing, reviews, optimised keywords, sales history.
- Maximum PPC investment funded by Oct–Dec sales income.
- If you exceed 40 sales/month: consider switching to **Amazon Professional (£25/month)**.
- If you have 10+ reviews and stable sales: consider activating **Amazon Vine** for more reviews.
