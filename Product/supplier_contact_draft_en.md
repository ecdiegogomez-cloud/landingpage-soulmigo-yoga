## PART 2: THE GRIP (Non-Slip Silicone Application)

### 2.1 Grip Design: "Anatomical Flow"

This is NOT a standard dot-pattern grip. We have developed a proprietary anatomical grip design that follows the biomechanics of the foot in yoga practice. We will provide CAD files.

### 2.2 Material & Thickness

| Specification | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Material** | 100% High-Density Silicone (eco-friendly, superior to PVC) |
| **Thickness** | **0.60mm - 0.80mm** (Tolerance: ±0.05mm) |
| **Finish** | Tone-on-tone (same color as the sock, slightly glossier/more saturated) or matte transparent |

> **Critical:** Thickness below 0.6mm causes premature wear and insufficient traction on damp surfaces. Thickness above 0.8mm causes loss of proprioception (ground feel), creating instability in balance poses.

### 2.3 Zone-Specific Dimensions

The grip is divided into 3 functional zones with different line widths and spacing:

| Zone | Location | Line Width | Spacing | Pattern | Function |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Metatarsal** | Ball of foot | **3.5mm** | **2.0mm** | Transverse waves | Maximizes push-off and braking. Lines follow phalangeal flexion axes. |
| **Plantar Arch** | Mid-foot | **1.8mm** | **3.0mm** | Longitudinal lines | Allows full arch flexibility. Acts as tensors preventing sock rotation during twists. |
| **Calcaneal** | Heel | **3.0mm** | — | "U" / Horseshoe shape | Lateral stabilization of the rearfoot. Cradles the calcaneus bone, centering load. |
