# Technical Composition Analysis: Yoga Socks (Premium Soft Touch)

### **78% Combed Cotton

* **Why 78%?** This high percentage classifies the sock as a "natural fiber rich" product. Unlike standard athletic socks (often 50-60% cotton), a 78% composition prioritizes **breathability** and **hypoallergenic comfort**. It allows for maximum air circulation, keeping the foot cool during practice.
* **Why "Combed"?** This is the critical differentiator for a "Soft Touch" label. The combing process removes short, prone-to-fraying fibers and aligns the remaining long fibers in parallel.
  * **Technical Benefit:** The removal of short fibers significantly reduces **pilling** (those small balls of fluff) after washing.
  * **Functional Benefit:** The aligned fibers create a smoother surface fabric, reducing friction against the skin and minimizing the risk of blisters during repetitive movements.
* **Moisture Management:** Cotton is hygroscopic (absorbs moisture). At 78%, it effectively absorbs light-to-medium sweat, preventing that "slippery" feeling inside the sock that can occur with 100% synthetic socks.

---

### **18-20% Polyester / Polyamide**

* **The Structural Skeleton:** While cotton provides comfort, it lacks structural memory. This synthetic component acts as the "skeleton" of the sock.
* **Durability:** Polyamide (Nylon) has exceptional tensile strength and abrasion resistance. It is strategically knitted into high-wear areas (heel and toe) to prevent holes, extending the product's lifespan significantly compared to pure cotton socks.
* **Moisture Wicking:** These hydrophobic fibers do not absorb water. Instead, they provide channels for moisture to move away from the skin toward the outer layer of the sock where it can evaporate. This counterbalances the cotton's absorption, ensuring the sock doesn't become heavy or soggy.
* **Dimensional Stability:** Prevents shrinkage after washing.

---

### **2-4% Spandex (Elastane)**

* **Elastic Recovery:** Spandex is an elastomeric polyurethane fiber that can stretch up to 500% and return to its original shape.
* **The "Perfect Fit":**
  * **At 2-4%:** This range provides sufficient compression to keep the sock snugly in place without constricting blood flow.
  * **Anti-Rotation:** In yoga, foot placement is precise. If a sock rotates (twists) around the foot, the grip pads on the bottom become misaligned, creating a safety hazard. The elastane ensures the sock clamps securely to the mid-foot arch, preventing this rotation.
* **Shape Retention:** Ensures the sock doesn't become baggy or loose ("bagging out") during a 60-minute session of stretching and flexing.

### **Reinforced Arch & Ankle**

* **Arch Support Band:** A denser knit of elastane around the mid-foot. This gently lifts the arch, reducing fatigue during standing balance poses and further locking the sock in place.
* **Ankle Tab/Cuff:** Light reinforcement here prevents the sock from sliding down into the shoe (if worn) or bunching up during inversions.
