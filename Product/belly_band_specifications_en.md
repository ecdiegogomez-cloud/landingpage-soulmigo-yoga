# Belly Band Specifications: Yoga Socks Label

This document defines the specifications for the belly band label used on the yoga socks product. The belly band serves as a universal labeling solution for both presentations: socks packaged inside the mesh bag and socks sold individually.

---

## 1. General Characteristics

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Format** | Wrap-around strip that holds and presents a folded pair of socks |
| **Material** | Recycled kraft cardstock (250-300 gsm) or matte textured paper |
| **Width** | Approx. 4-5 cm (enough for logo + essential info) |
| **Closure** | Sealed with a small dot of eco-friendly adhesive or a circular sticker featuring the logo |
| **Color Palette** | Natural kraft background or Green `#5B6F55` with typography in Charcoal `#36454F` |

---

## 2. Front Side (Branding)

The front side is the brand-facing, visual side. It should be clean, minimal, and premium.

**Required elements:**

* **Logo:** The interlocking hands 'S' logo, centered.
* **Product Name:** "Yoga Socks" or equivalent product identifier.
* **Optional Tagline:** A short brand statement if applicable (e.g., "Move with purpose").

---

## 3. Back Side (Regulatory & Informational)

The back side contains all legally required and value-added information.

### 3.1 Mandatory Information (Textile Regulations)

These fields are required by law for textile products sold in the EU (Reg. 1007/2011) and the USA (FTC Textile Rules):

| Field | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Fiber Composition** | Exact percentage of each fiber, listed in descending order. | `75% Organic Cotton, 20% Polyamide, 5% Elastane` |
| **Size** | Clear and visible. Can be numeric (EU: 36-39) or alphabetic (S/M/L). | `Size: M (39-42)` |
| **Country of Manufacture** | "Made in..." is mandatory in the USA and highly recommended in the EU. | `Made in Portugal` |
| **Care Instructions** | In the EU, use GINETEX symbols (universal wash care icons). In the USA, written instructions are also accepted. | Icons: Wash 30°, Do not bleach, Tumble dry low, Do not iron |
| **Manufacturer / Importer ID** | Company name and address of the responsible entity. Mandatory in the EU for B2C sales. | `Soulmigo S.L. — Barcelona, Spain` |

### 3.2 Recommended Information (Added Value)

These are not legally required but significantly elevate perceived quality and aid operations:

| Field | Why Include It |
|---|---|
| **Certifications** | If the cotton is GOTS, OEKO-TEX, or GRS (for rPET), display the seal. This provides enormous credibility to eco-conscious consumers. |
| **QR Code** | Link to the website, a product page with extended info, or a "how to use" video. Very useful since the belly band has limited space. |
| **Barcode / EAN** | Essential if selling in physical retail stores or on marketplaces like Amazon. |
| **Internal SKU** | For inventory management, in small text. |
| **Sustainability Claim** | A short statement such as: "Packaged in a reusable & washable mesh bag" or "100% Organic Cotton". This reinforces the premium-eco positioning. |
| **Pack Contents** | Indicate what the pack includes. This is where the two presentations differ. |

### 3.3 Pack Contents (The Only Difference Between Versions)

To keep production simple, the belly band is identical for both presentations except for one single line:

| Presentation | Pack Contents Line |
|---|---|
| **With Mesh Bag** | `1 Pair Yoga Socks + 1 Washable Mesh Bag` |
| **Without Mesh Bag** | `1 Pair Yoga Socks` |
