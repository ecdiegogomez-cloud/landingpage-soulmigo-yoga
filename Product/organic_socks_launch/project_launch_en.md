## 7. Immediate Action Items

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Critical Steps to verify before launch:**
>
> 1. **Verify Material**: Ask supplier: *"Is the 100% cotton referring to the whole sock or just the yarn? What is the Elastane/Spandex %?"* (100% cotton socks fall down; you need at least 2-3% elastane).
> 2. **Confirm Packaging Dimensions**: Ensure packed bundle is **< 2.5cm thick** to qualify for Royal Mail "Large Letter" (£1.85). If > 2.5cm, cost rises to Small Parcel (~£3.29).
> 3. **Request GOTS Certificate**: Essential for verifying the "Organic" claim in your marketing.
