## Logo Application Methods

### Screen Printing ✅ Recommended

- **Cost:** $0.05 - $0.15 per bag (volume dependent)
- **Durability:** Excellent (50+ washes)
- **Quality:** Sharp, professional finish
- **MOQ:** Typically 500+ units
- **Best for:** High volume production

### Embroidery

- **Cost:** $0.20 - $0.40 per bag
- **Durability:** Excellent (100+ washes)
- **Quality:** Premium, tactile feel
- **MOQ:** Typically 250+ units
- **Best for:** Premium positioning

### Heat Transfer

- **Cost:** $0.10 - $0.20 per bag
- **Durability:** Good (30-40 washes)
- **Quality:** Good for simple logos
- **MOQ:** Lower (100+ units)
- **Best for:** Small batch testing

## Market Analysis: Suitability for Female Audience

Based on **Premium Activewear trends (2025-2026)** and color psychology in the wellness sector, this palette is **highly effective** for women for the following reasons:

### 1. White and Beige (The "Clean & Mindful" Aesthetic)

- **Why it works:** These are the dominant colors in Yoga and Pilates studios. They convey cleanliness, calm, and a focus on natural materials (organic cotton).
- **Trend:** The "Organic Minimalist" look is currently the #1 trend for leaders like Alo Yoga or Lululemon.

### 2. Charcoal and Moss Green (The "Earth & Technical" Vibe)

- **Why it works:** **Moss Green** is extremely popular right now because it's perceived as a "sophisticated neutral." It's not a generic green; it conveys balance and nature.
- **Practicality:** **Charcoal** is highly valued by users for its practicality: it hides wear and maintains a sleek look longer than white.

### 3. Premium Positioning vs. Mass Retail

- **Avoid:** Generic "neon" or "bright pinks," which are often associated with lower quality or "fast-fashion" products.
- **Success Strategy:** Use earth-toned, desaturated, and matte finishes. This palette communicates that the product is a **piece of technical equipment**, not just a fashion accessory.

> [!TIP]
> If you want to expand the line with something "softer," consider **Dusty Rose** or **Sage**, keeping the tones desaturated to maintain the premium feel.
