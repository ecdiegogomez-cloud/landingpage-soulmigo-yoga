# Packaging Specifications: Mesh Bag (Yoga Socks)

This document details the technical characteristics, suggested materials, and the psychology behind the color options developed for the premium packaging of the yoga socks.

---

## 1. General Packaging Characteristics

* **Style:** Drawstring Bag.
* **Main Body:** Open mesh that allows product ventilation (ideal for post-use sports/yoga socks) and lets the fabric breathe.
* **Branding Positioning:**
  * **Label:** Small Woven Label sewn into the **upper right** side seam, just below the drawstring channel.
  * **Logo:** The interlocking hands ('S') logo must be integrated into the woven label, not printed directly on the mesh. This ensures long-term durability (it won't fade or peel when the bag is washed) and drastically elevates the perceived value of the product.
  * **Size:** Subtle and minimalist.

---

## 2. Suggested Materials

To maintain the "premium" and "eco-friendly" positioning projected by the designs:

### For the Bag Body (The Mesh)

* **Option A (Most premium/eco): Organic Cotton Mesh.** Provides a soft, natural, and matte feel. It is biodegradable and breathes exceptionally well.
* **Option B (High durability): Recycled Polyester (rPET).** If the bag is designed primarily as a "wash bag" to be put in the washing machine, rPET resists constant friction better than pure cotton, maintaining the ecological aspect by using recycled plastics.

### For the Label (Woven Label)

* **Cotton Twill or Taffeta:** To achieve a smooth texture where the contrasting logo (black, charcoal, etc.) stands out with high-density threads for maximum definition of the logo's lines.

### For the Drawstring

* **Round Braided Cotton:** Should be a 100% natural, thick cotton cord (approx. 5mm - 7mm) so the knot at the ends looks robust and high-quality. Avoid shiny nylon cords, as they visually cheapen the packaging.

---

## 3. Color Psychology and Contrasts (Developed Options)

The secret of these designs lies in **anchoring contrast**: using earth or pastel colors for the soft body of the bag, and anchoring them with dark/strong colors in the drawstrings and logo to give it character and modernity.

### Beige & Black Option (The Organic Classic)

* **Colors:** Raw Beige Bag + Pure Black Drawstring/Logo.
* **The Why:** This is the gold standard in sustainable products. Beige communicates purity, the absence of toxic dyes, and nature. The pure black in the drawstrings cuts through that softness, giving it a defined frame and an air of urban luxury (boutique gym style).

### Earth Green & Charcoal Option (The Natural Athletic)

* **Colors:** Green Bag `#5B6F55` + Charcoal Gray Drawstring/Logo `#36454F`.
* **The Why:** This green refers to health, wellness, and matcha (very aligned with yoga). Instead of using black (which would be too harsh), charcoal gray softens the contrast while maintaining legibility. It is ideal if you want to position the brand towards vitality and connection with nature.

### Charcoal & Green Option (The Modern/Masculine-Unisex)

* **Colors:** Charcoal Gray Bag `#36454F` + Green Drawstring/Logo `#5B6F55`.
* **The Why:** By darkening the main bag, the packaging immediately becomes more sober, "tech," and modern. It is excellent if the target market includes men or if the brand seeks to project resistance and durability. The green touch on the drawstring and logo prevents it from looking boring.

### Dusty Rose & Charcoal Option (The Elegant Feminine)

* **Colors:** Dusty Rose Bag `#BFAFB2` + Charcoal Gray Drawstring/Logo `#36454F`.
* **The Why:** Dusty rose conveys calm, self-care, and mature femininity (avoiding childish "bubblegum" pink). By anchoring this soft color with dark (charcoal) drawstrings, you prevent the packaging from looking cheap or flimsy. The dark contrast immediately gives it visual weight and a sophisticated air (Premium Wellness).
