# Research: Washable Bags for Sock Packaging (1 Pair)

**Date:** February 17, 2026
**Related Product:** SB10365 — Reformer Trainer Gripper Socks
**Objective:** Find washable mesh bags with capacity for **one pair of socks**, suitable as reusable and functional packaging.

---

## 1. Recommended Size

For a single pair of yoga/pilates socks, the ideal bag size is:

| Size | Dimensions | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Extra Small** | 10 × 12 cm (~4" × 5") | Very tight fit, ideal for compact presentation |
| **Small** | 15 × 20 cm (~6" × 8") | **Recommended** — sufficient space for pair + room for washing |
| **Medium** | 20 × 25 cm (~8" × 10") | Loose fit, more versatile but less premium as packaging |

> [!TIP]
> The **15 × 20 cm** size offers the best balance: compact enough to look premium as packaging, yet functional for washing the socks inside the bag.

---

## 2. Materials and Key Features

| Feature | Recommended Specification |
|:---|:---|
| **Material** | Polyester or nylon mesh (durable, breathable) |
| **Mesh hole size** | 2-3 mm max (prevents small items from escaping) |
| **Closure** | Drawstring or zipper |
| **Washable** | Yes — machine washable, dryer safe |
| **Reusable** | Yes — designed for multiple wash cycles |
| **Custom logo** | Available with most wholesale suppliers |

---

## 3. Closure Options

### Drawstring ✅ Recommended for Packaging

- More organic and premium look
- Compatible with branding (logo on fabric or tag)
- More economical to produce
- Dual function: elegant packaging + laundry bag

### Zipper

- More secure closure for washing
- More functional/technical look
- Slightly higher cost
- Risk of rust if not quality (rust-free required)

## 5. Added Value as Packaging

Using a washable bag as product packaging has strategic advantages:

1. **Dual function** → Customer receives packaging + useful accessory
2. **Sustainability** → Eliminates disposable packaging (aligned with eco profile of product with 81% organic cotton)
3. **Differentiation** → Few competitors use mesh bags as primary packaging
4. **Branding** → Embroidered or printed logo on bag = continuous advertising
5. **Protection** → Socks with silicone grippers stay protected
