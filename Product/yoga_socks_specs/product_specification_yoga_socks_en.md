# Product Specification: Premium Yoga Socks

This document details the technical and design specifications for our new yoga socks product, targeting the premium female market.

## 1. Composition & Materials

To achieve the perfect balance of breathability, durability, and elasticity, a high-quality blend of natural and synthetic fibers will be used.

* **Suggested Composition:**
  * **78% Combed Cotton:** For maximum softness, comfort, and moisture absorption.
  * **18-20% Polyester/Polyamide:** For durability, wear resistance, and quick drying.
  * **2-4% Spandex (Elastane):** For a perfect fit, elasticity, and shape retention.
* **Texture:** Soft Touch fabric with light reinforcement in the arch and ankle.

## 2. Design & Construction

The design must be minimalist and functional, strictly adhering to the height restriction to maintain an elegant and discreet aesthetic.

* **Height/Profile:** Low Cut / No-Show / Ankle.
  * **Critical Restriction:** The cuff height or top band must not exceed **2.5 cm** from the ankle bone, or the elastic band width must not exceed 2.5 cm to maintain a stylized profile.
* **Style:** Minimalist, Seamless Toe to prevent chafing.
* **Ventilation:** Breathable mesh panel on the instep to improve airflow.

## 3. Color Palette (Earthy & Subtle)

Nature-inspired colors, subtle and elegant to stand out without being loud.

* **"Earthy Serenity" Collection:**
    1. **Moss Green:** A soft, deep greyish-green. (#5B6F55)
    2. **Warm Sand:** A neutral and warm beige, very versatile. (#D2B48C)
    3. **Stone Grey:** A medium grey with warm undertones. (#9CA3A8)
    4. **Dusty Rose:** A very subtle and muted feminine touch. (#BFAFB2)
    5. **Charcoal:** A nearly black grey for the classic basic option. (#36454F)

## 4. Grips (Non-Slip)

The grip system is fundamental for safe yoga and pilates practice.

* **Material:** 100% High-density Silicone (eco-friendly and durable). Superior to PVC in grip and longevity.
* **Format/Pattern:**
  * **Full Sole Coverage:** Uniform distribution of high-density silicone dots covering the entire sole (heel, arch, and metatarsals).
  * Provides consistent traction and stability across the entire footprint.
* **Grip Color:** "Tone-on-tone" (same color as the sock but glossier/more saturated) or matte transparent/white to not interfere with the design.
* **Thickness:** Low profile to avoid discomfort when stepping, but sufficient for effective traction.

## 5. Sizes (Women's)

Based on international standards to ensure universal fit. As the material is stretchy, they are grouped in ranges.

| Size | EU Equivalent | UK Equivalent | US Equivalent |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S** | 35 - 38 | 3 - 5 | 5 - 7 |
| **M** | 39 - 42 | 6 - 8 | 7.5 - 9.5 |
| **L** | 43+ | 9+ | 10+ |

*Note: Size M typically has the highest sales volume.*
